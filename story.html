TAHO+ — Our Story

Our
Story

A brand born from a love for tradition and a passion for innovation.

Every morning across the Philippines, the call of the magtataho echoes through the streets. That familiar voice, that warm cup of silken tofu, sweet arnibal, and sago pearls — it's more than a snack. It's a memory. A ritual. A piece of Filipino identity.

TAHO+ was born from a deep love for that tradition. We asked ourselves: what if we could take that feeling — that comfort, that nostalgia — and give it a modern frozen twist? What if the treat you grew up with could become something entirely new, yet instantly familiar?

Inspired by the beloved Filipino street treat, we've reimagined taho into a refreshing frozen experience. Whether it's your first time tasting taho or a return to a childhood favorite — we deliver moments of joy in every cup.

TAHO+ is made for today's adventurous eaters. People who crave something nostalgic yet brand new. Something familiar, but exciting.
