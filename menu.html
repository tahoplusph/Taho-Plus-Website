TAHO+ — Our Menu

Our
Menu

Six signature frozen taho creations — each one rooted in Filipino flavor, reimagined as a modern frozen treat. Plus our self-serve Swirl Your Own bar at select locations.

Signature Flavors
Classic TAHO+

The original. Taho soft serve drizzled with arnibal, topped with sago pearls and black tapioca. Where it all started.

Cookie TAHO+

Cookies and cream meets frozen taho. Crushed Oreo crumbles over taho soft serve with black tapioca.

Golden Trio

Honey-kissed taho soft serve crowned with premium mixed nuts and sago pearls. Sweet, nutty, and beautifully elevated.

Mango TAHO+

Sun-ripened mango chunks and arnibal swirled through our taho soft serve with black tapioca and Lengua de Gato.

Mi Ultimo

Our most loaded cup. Honey, mixed nuts, fresh fruits, and black pearls — the ultimate TAHO+ experience.

Strawberry TAHO+

Vibrant strawberry taho soft serve with fresh strawberries, black tapioca pearls, and Lengua de Gato.
