07 — FAQ

Questions

TAHO+ takes the beloved Filipino street treat — silken tofu, sago pearls, and arnibal syrup — and reimagines it as a creamy frozen soft serve. It's a refreshing, modern experience rooted in a flavor every Filipino already knows and loves.

Our Frozen Taho is made with a taho-based soft serve that promotes wellness without sacrificing taste. It's a lighter, plant-based alternative to traditional frozen desserts — and a delicious one at that.

We currently operate kiosks across Metro Manila. Our goal is to be the most trusted Frozen Taho brand in the local market, with more locations opening throughout 2025. Check our Locations section for the nearest store.

Visit our Franchise section and submit an inquiry. We're looking for partners who share our vision of bringing traditional Pinoy flavors to modern consumers. Our team will follow up within 5–7 business days.

Our base product contains soy (tofu). Certain seasonal flavors may include additional ingredients — all clearly labeled at our kiosks. Please let our staff know if you have any dietary concerns and we'll be happy to assist.
